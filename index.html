About Biostat Stars

Biostat Stars (BSS), founded by Dr. Nwamaka Bob-Ume in 2017, is an e-learning platform dedicated to providing a fun and effective learning experience in biostatistics for students and healthcare professionals.

BSS supports university courses, medical residencies, and exams such as IFOM, NBME, USMLE, AMC MCQ, and PLAB. Its resources, including video lectures, concept notes, and a question bank of over 380 board-style questions, facilitate asynchronous learning. To date, over 170 healthcare students and doctors worldwide have benefited from Biostat Stars.
