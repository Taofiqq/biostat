About Biostat Stars

Biostat Stars (BSS) is an e-learning platform founded by Dr. Nwamaka Bob-Ume in 2017 with the mission to equip students and healthcare professionals with the most enjoyable learning experience in biostatistics.

BSS is useful for university courses, medical residency programs and professional exams like IFOM, NBME, USMLE, AMC MCQ and PLAB.
BSS services include video lectures, concept notes and a question bank with over 370 board-style questions for asynchronous learning. Over 100 healthcare students and doctors have benefitted from Biostat Stars globally.
